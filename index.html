The Jitterbug Story

Every jitterbug has a back-story!

  • 2009-2011

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The Jitterbug Coffee Shop began with a simple idea: People need great coffee. We opened up shop with that philosophy, selling only the basics.

  • 2011 - 2012

    Moving On Up

    We opened up a brand new storefront and started getting more traffic - and more ideas! Customers wanted us to open earlier and close later. They wanted teas, pastries, and sandwiches, and we complied!

  • 2012 - 2014

    Growing... And Growing...

    We continued to listen, and continued to grow. During 2013, we began hosting events and selling books. In 2014, we opened a second location.

  • 2014 - 2016

    Reaching for the Stars!

    In 2015 we began selling coffee accoutrements, like travel mugs and French presses. In 2016, we plan to sell our wares online, not just in stores. Who knows where our journey will lead us next?! Either way, we'd love for you to stop by and...

Pastries

Fresh-baked pastries, from our local bakery.

We buy our pastries fresh every morning from a local bakery just down the street. We make sure they only use fresh, organic, delicious ingredients, for our favorite customers!

Have a favorite pastry that we don't provide? Let us know, and we will work with our bakery to make sure you get your fix!

Coffee Drinks

Everything under the sun!

We are proud to say that our world-class baristas have a variety of drinks under their belts- however, if we don't offer something that you're craving, feel free to make a request! We always love finding new favorites.

Teas, Please

Teas and Specialty Drinks

In addition to coffee, we now offer free trade, organic teas in a variety of flavors. Alongside the traditional blends, you can find a special of the month at both locations.

Teas are served loose leaf, so we encourage you to sit a while to enjoy your blend. However, if you're in a hurry, we can transfer tea to small bags- or even sell you a larger amount to make at home!

Lunch and Brunch

Meals for Hungry Bugs

From 11am to close, we are now offering sandwiches, salads, soups and a hot special of the day, alongside our traditional coffees, teas and pastries.

You are welcome to take your treats to go, but we've also added seating in both of our locations for those of you who'd like to stay!
